About Us

What is Classify?

In the wide world of education, each class has a different style of learning, and each style of learning requires the right tools for the job. Some classes task its students with using instant-feedback math learning websites, some implement interactive writing helpers, and others require graphic design platforms. However, what happens when the amount of tools and websites students have to use simply becomes too much?

Enter Classify, an all-encompassing learning platform that allows students to view and submit files, assignments and projects from one secure location. With current support for Canvas©, zyBooks© and Cengage©, and much more expanded support on the way, Classify aims to free the overwhelmed student of copious bookmarks and ease them into our virtual gateway to unhindered learning.

Do I need a Classify account?

Nope, there is no Classify account required to use our services! As a gateway platform, simply signing into your learning websites from the Classify portal will allow you to access all of your assignments and data. Additionally, Classify will never send emails or messages to you unless you specifically request them from our Contact Us page.

What websites have Classify support?

Classify currently allows access to Canvas© assignments, zyBooks© and Cengage©. However, support is planned for other learning platforms such as Google Classroom©, Macmillan Learning Achieve©, and more. In addition, development is currently underway for fully integrated Canvas© support! Join our mailing list for updates on new releases and features.

Does Classify require a paid subscription?

No, Classify does not require any form of paid subscription and will not request any fees or payments. Classify aims to provide all of the high-quality services of a centralized learning platform at no cost to its users.

Will Classify store my data?

No, Classify will never store any personal or activity data from users. Because Classify does not have its own account system, user data is never stored, distributed, or recalled to alter user experience. The only data that Classify collects is that provided by users to connect with the Classify team. For more information regarding our privacy policy, email us at theclassifyteam@gmail.com.
